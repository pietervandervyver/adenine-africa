Our Research Focus

Adenine Research is a Computational Neuroscience company based in Africa, dedicated to advancing cochlear implant technology through the use of optical fibers in place of conventional metal electrodes. Our work combines biophysical modelling, neural signal processing, and photonics to design implants that deliver greater frequency resolution and reduced neural damage compared to existing devices. We believe that Africa has a unique role to play in driving this research forward, given the continent's significant burden of hearing loss and its growing scientific talent. By grounding our work in rigorous computational methods, we aim to translate laboratory findings into clinical solutions that improve quality of life for people with severe hearing impairment. Our long-term goal is to establish Africa as a centre of excellence for neuroprosthetics research and development.
